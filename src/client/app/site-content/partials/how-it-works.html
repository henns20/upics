Calendar Sessions

Affordable, High Production Value, & Inspired

The calendared session is an option that aims to maximize quality, inspiration, & affordability.

What are calendared sessions?

Calendared sessions are fixed windows of time that we set aside during the week in which we offer fixed amount of slots or openings. the benefit to the individual customer who signs up for a calendar session is that they get charged a less-expensive group rate.

How can you sign up for a calendar session?

Calendar sessions are offered for any of the Silicon Valley / Tech inspired pics included in the gallery. When choosing a particular pic offering there will be a calendar on the sign up portion of the page. The dates that are scheduled for a calendared session will be highlighted in green. When you choose 1 of those days it will then offer times that are open. Select the time that works for you and is open. Then proceed to click the button Book Now.
