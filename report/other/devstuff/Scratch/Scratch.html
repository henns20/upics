Calendered Sessions

Calendar sessions are a days or other limited period of times during the week that we make plans to be open and to have multiple pre-crafted production setups from our gallery available. We do this as one part to optimize affordability for the individual customers

How to sign up for calendared sessions.

To sign up for calendar sessions you only need to go to the profile pic details page. There is a drop-down menu that will indicate the calendar session times that are open. Just choose Time that best works for you. Once you've done that click book now button.

Dynamic countered session times

Encountered session times may change from week to week to keep people up to date an to notify our latest calendar session schedules we use social media apps like Twitter to broadcast do Them. You may also sign up to our email List to get it that way as well.

Flex Appointments

Flex appointments is another way to book or get a profile pics - specifically the pre-crafted and inspired work since Silicon Valley tech inspired pics that we offer. With flex appointments people can book the pre-crafted inspired profile pics from our gallery outside of the calendar session time. A time that is more convenient to you.

How to sign up for flex appointment

To sign up for flux appointment go to the pick details page; choose the flex appointment drop-down list choose a time. You may also call or email us directly in order to schedule or book the flex appointment.
